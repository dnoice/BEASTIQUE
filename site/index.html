The Project

Art Meets Evidence

BEASTIQUE renders endangered creatures in the precious metals, industrial minerals, and rare substances we destroy their habitats to extract. Every image is a transaction receipt. Every fact is sourced and citeable.

This is not protest art. It is a translation layer between peer-reviewed ecological data and the human capacity to care. If someone challenges a claim, the receipts are one click away. If someone asks what can actually be done, the experience responds with documented interventions that work.

A digiSpace Studios Production

Each piece in the BEASTIQUE collection is the product of a meticulous process: custom SVG frameworks built from scratch in Inkscape, layered with photographic reference and digital enhancement techniques refined over 18 months. A single image can take anywhere from several hours to several days to complete.

The result is not illustration. It is not photography. It is a new form of conservation art that exists at the intersection of precision craft and ecological accountability.
