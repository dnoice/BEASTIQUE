Reptiles are the oldest blueprints still in print. They watched the great dyings come and go — the ash, the cold, the drowned world — and they kept the design running for a quarter of a billion years.

Now the design is being shredded by hand. Habitat cleared, wetlands drained, the skin trade and the pet trade pulling the rarest first. The leatherback has crossed oceans since the dinosaurs and now dies on a longline or a beach paved for a resort.

BEASTIQUE renders them in the materials of the trade — tooled leather for the animal reduced to a handbag, a wallet, a belt. Beauty is the argument. This is the oldest thing we are throwing away.
