## Tiers

- **Tier 1 — definite.** Exact synonyms and generic-vs-specific redundancies. Same animal, or a bare generic when named exemplars already exist.
- **Tier 2 — strong.** Form collapse: within a reduced style you'd be hard-pressed to tell these from the keeper. Judgment, but confident.
- **Tier 3 — your call.** Form collapse where a conservation story argues for keeping both. Flagged, not assumed.
